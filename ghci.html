Grace Hopper Celebration India, 2019


The Grace Hopper Celebration (India) is India’s largest gathering of women technologists. It is produced by AnitaB.org and presented in partnership with ACM India.

On 25 October 2019, I received a phone call asking me if I knew about Anita B.org. I had recenlty attended GHC'19 and knew fully about it. I jumped with excitement and told the lady, "Yes, I know everything about it!" She then told me that Google was willing to fund my travel and stay for attending the conference. I was very excited to hear the news!

It was a three day long conference from 6 to 8 November, all days jam packed with a variety of events all day long. It was the 10th anniversary of GHCI and the celebration was grader this time. Mornings stared with Keynote sessions, proceeded with numerous technical and hands-on lab sessions, and ended with us visiting company booths at the Career Fair. Snacks and lunch provided at the venue were a foodie’s paradise. One of my favourite sessions was the Speed Mentoring. We gathered in groups, asked questions to and were guided by expertised women in technology. The session was truly a motivation-booster.



Google had arranged for our stay at the Golden Palm Resorts, where about 20 of us were staying together. Days passed with us being at the venue attending the conference, while evenings and nights were spent bonding and learning with friends. I made great friends and have memories that I'll cherish forever!
