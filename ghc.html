Grace Hopper Celebration, 2019


Grace Hopper Celebration is the largest gathering for Women in Technology and is celebrated every year to bring the research and career interests of women in computing to the forefront.

It was May 8, 2019 and I was in Bangalore during that time for my summer internship at Mathworks. I received a mail saying that I have been awarded a GHC Student Scholarship to attend the 2019 Grace Hopper Celebration in Orlando, Florida from October 1 to 4. It was an unbelievabe. A series of emotions rushed through me as I was reading the email. I had been indeed been selected for it and was among the three Indians selected for it!

The D-day had come and I was ready to fly to Orlando on 30th October, 2019. It was a 4 day long conference held at the Orange County C onvention Centre, Orlando. This time there were about 25000 women expected to arrive, and the crowd could be seen as soon as I entered the hall from where we were supposed to collect our badges. Going to GHC was exhilarating and revitalizing in so many ways! There were numerous events lined up from morning till evening and a variety of parties hosted by companies at night. There were Keynotes, mentoring sessions, tech talks, one on one interactions, hackathons, award series, leadership summits, poster sessions, open source sessions and ofcourse, the world's largest career fair! Days used to begin with an early morning breakfast with random people at brealfast booths, and end with huge pots of ideas and memories. These were one of the best days of my lives! I am so thankful to Anita B.org for proving me with this scholarship!



GHC provided me an opportunity to meet with and build a collaborative network with successful women from across the globe who have carved their niches in technology. The community will act as constant support to help myself and others to overcome the feeling of isolation and be a source of inspiration and motivation to work harder. I am very happy to say that GHC is indeed a highlight of my life!
