About

I am a final year student, currently pursuing my bachelors in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at IIIT Delhi.

I am passionate about emerging technologies and fascinated by how easy computers make our lives - ranging from education and sports to design and medical assistance. My work primarily focuses on designing applications using the medium of Augmented and Virtual Reality for the societal good. I wish to expand my horizon to new and diverse ideas that people are working on.

I am a part of the Weave Lab, under Dr. Aman Parnami and Melange Research Lab, under Dr. Pushpendra Singh.
